The problem!

Those of us who recycle have stumbled upon the following problem: we don't know what happens to the waste after it has been collected. We can't know for sure whether it reaches the recycling center or it's unloaded at the garbage dump.

Our solution!

Our solution offers a real-time tracking of the whole recycling process. Using our app you can quantify your contribution to the recycling process and, at the same time, secure the transparency of it, such that you minimize the recycling waste.
